Shipping & Pickup

Everything you need to know about getting your fresh-baked treats.

Pickup Service

Pick up your fresh-baked order at either of our convenient locations. We offer a wide variety of freshly baked cookies with unique flavors and high-quality ingredients, including frosted sugar cookies, chunky chocolate chip cookies, red velvet cookies, and thumbprint heart cookies that can be personalized for any occasion.

Delivery

We offer delivery in the Frisco and Austin areas so you can enjoy fresh-baked treats right at your door. Delivery fees may apply depending on your location. Contact us for delivery details and availability in your area.

How It Works

  1. Place Your Order — Browse our menu and submit your order at least 5 days in advance. Late orders (2-3 days before) incur a $10 fee.
  2. We Confirm Details — Allow 1-2 days for us to confirm availability, discuss specifics, and finalize your order.
  3. Submit Your Deposit — Pay your 100% deposit via Venmo, Zelle, or Credit/Debit to confirm your order.
  4. Pick Up or Receive Delivery — Collect your fresh-baked treats at the scheduled time, or receive them via delivery!

Important Notes

  • Orders must be picked up at the scheduled time and date.
  • If you cannot make your pickup, please notify us in advance.
  • Unclaimed orders without prior notification may be redistributed.
  • All products are baked fresh — we recommend enjoying them within 3-5 days.
