Store Policy

Everything you need to know about ordering from Sweet Delish Bakery.

Ordering Process

Welcome to Sweet Delish Bakery in Frisco, Texas, where we specialize in crafting personalized cookies for every occasion. To ensure a seamless experience, please follow our ordering process:

  • Lead Time Requirements: Kindly place your order requests at least 5 days in advance.
  • Late Orders: Please note that orders made 2-3 days before pickup will incur an additional $10 fee.

Confirmation and Payment

After submitting the order request form, allow 1-2 days for us to confirm availability and discuss specifics. Your order is considered verified only upon receipt of a 100% deposit.

  • Payment Options: Venmo, Zelle, & Credit/Debit.
  • Deposit Requirement: A 100% deposit is necessary to confirm your order.
  • Order Verification: Orders are considered verified only upon receipt of the full deposit.
  • Cancellation Policy: Be advised that we reserve the right to redistribute your cookies without a refund if not picked up at the scheduled time and date without prior notification.
  • We do not provide refunds.

Product Details and Customization

Rest assured, our cookies are crafted with fresh, high-quality ingredients that are 100% halal/kosher. If you have a specific design in mind, please follow these guidelines:

  • Design Specifications: Share images and provide precise instructions for colors, styles, writing, and decorations.
  • Color Accuracy: While we strive to match your vision, please understand that color accuracy may not be 100% guaranteed.
  • Allergy Notice: Our cookies contain the following allergens: eggs, nuts, and wheat.

Communication and Finalization

Once you've filled out the order form, we'll reach out to you to discuss details and availability before finalizing the order.

  • Communication Process: Expect us to reach out to discuss details and availability after submitting the order form.
  • Contact Information: For faster service and direct pricing information, feel free to contact us at sweetdelishbakery@gmail.com
